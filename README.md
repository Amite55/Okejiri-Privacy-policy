# 🛡️ Privacy Policy for Okejiri

**Effective Date:** April 20, 2026

## 📋 Overview
This Privacy Policy applies to the **Okejiri** Application ("Application"), a service provider platform designed to connect users with independent service providers and established companies for booking and delivering various services.

By using Okejiri, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 👤 User Roles & Data Hierarchy
Okejiri supports a multi-tier user structure:
- **User (Customer):** Individuals who book and receive services.
- **Service Provider:** Independent professionals delivering services.
- **Company:** Organizations that manage a team of employees.
- **Employee:** Staff members working under a Company account. *Note: Service-related data, performance, and real-time location of employees are shared with their respective parent Company for operational management.*

---

## 📊 Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
- Full Name, Email Address, Phone Number, Profile Image, and Physical Address.

### 2. Authentication Data
- Credentials for Email/Password login and Google Sign-In metadata.

### 3. KYC (Know Your Customer) Information
- To ensure platform safety, we collect National ID (NID), Passport, or other official government documents to verify the identity of Users and Providers.

### 4. Location Data
- **Real-time Location:** We collect precise location data to enable service tracking and delivery. This data may be collected even when the app is running in the background to ensure accurate tracking during an active service session.

### 5. Communication Data
- Messaging logs between Users and Providers are collected only during active or pending orders. Access to these logs is restricted once an order is marked as complete.

### 6. Media & Device Data
- **Gallery Access:** To upload profile pictures or service-related proof.
- **Technical Data:** Device model, IP address, and Push Notification tokens (FCM) for real-time alerts.

---

## 🎯 How We Use Information
- To facilitate service bookings and identity verification (KYC).
- To track service delivery via location data for safety and accuracy.
- To manage service extensions, reports, and disputes.
- To enable the **Admin Appeal** process for resolving conflicts between parties.

---

## 🔄 Data Sharing & Security
- **Data Sharing:** We share data between Users and Providers solely to fulfill bookings. We **do not sell or rent** your personal data to third parties.
- **Payments:** Transactions are processed via secure third-party gateways (Stripe/Cryptocurrency). We do **not** store sensitive card or bank details.
- **Security:** We implement industry-standard encryption to protect your KYC and personal data.

---

## ⚖️ Disputes, Reports & Appeals
- Users can report providers or initiate a dispute if a service is unsatisfactory.
- Our Admin team reviews communication logs and data to settle disputes.
- All users have the right to appeal an admin decision through the internal appeal system.

---

## ❌ Account & Data Deletion
We respect your right to be forgotten:
- **In-App Deletion:** Users can delete their account by navigating to **Profile > Settings > Delete Account**.
- **Data Removal:** Upon deletion, all personal data is removed from our active databases, except where retention is legally required (e.g., financial transaction records).

---

## 🌍 Third-Party Services
We utilize:
- **Google Sign-In:** For authentication.
- **Firebase:** For push notifications and real-time database management.
- **Stripe/Crypto Gateways:** For secure payment processing.
